Saksham's Movie Recommendation

Here are the 3 top movies according to me

Swades

It is about an indian guy who come back to india after working in Nasa in the United States. He designs solutions for the villagers in his ancestral village and realize how important it is to give back to his home country

Chak de India

This is the story of an underdog team; Indian women hockey team that went on to win the world cup. The biggest role behind this was the inspiration and hardwork by the coach

3 Idiots

This is a story of 3 engineering friends. One of the guy out of these was a genius and had a completely different outlook from other engineering students from the batch and focussed on skill instead of going into the rat race of high salaries

Unbroken

This is a story of olymic runner who got stuck in Japanese confinement and was tortured by japanese war criminal - the Bird
